Welcome to the Humans of Align Interactive Storytelling Website!

This Website has been developed to showcase the journeys of your fellow MS Aligners, throughout their time in the program, in an interactive visual format.

Humans of Align includes stories of students who have completed or are currently at different stages in the program, showing their own individual academic journey, informed by discoveries and decisions made along the way whilst leveraging their non-traditional backgrounds and expertise.

The goal of this website, by telling the stories of students in the MS Align programs as they explore their paths, find their passions, and acquire the skills and knowledge that prepare them to transition effectively into technology, is to highlights how each story is unique and comes from one person's experience. We hope you find the individual stories relatable, to demonstrate all the ways in which students can navigate Northeasternâ€™s endless options to create their own distinct journeys and break into technology.
